Welcome to Oslomet

Oslomet is a public university in Norway that offers a wide range of academic programs and research activities in various fields of study, such as health, social sciences, education, technology, art, and design. OsloMet is a diverse, inclusive, modern, and innovative university that focuses on social responsibility and sustainable development. OsloMet is a university that contributes to a more sustainable welfare state and a better society for everyone. Join us and discover your potential at OsloMet!
